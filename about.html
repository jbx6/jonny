About Jonny

Jonny is a drug safety app that helps you stay safe while using drugs. It provides information on how to use drugs safely, what to do in case of an overdose, and how to access naloxone. Jonny also allows you to share your location with a trusted contact in case of an emergency.

Jonny is designed to be a non-judgmental and supportive tool for people who use drugs. It is not intended to replace professional medical advice or treatment, but to provide harm reduction information and resources to help keep you safe.

Jonny is free to use and does not require any personal information to access. It is available on the web and can be used on any device with an internet connection.
